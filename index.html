Authentic Home Cooking Classes in Accra, Ghana - What to Expect

Market Tour & Ingredient Shopping in Accra (Optional)

Your Ghanaian cooking adventure begins at bustling Accra markets, where you'll learn to select fresh ingredients for traditional Ghanaian dishes just like local families do. Experience authentic market culture while sourcing spices, vegetables, and proteins essential to genuine Ghanaian home cooking. Our market tour teaches you about Ghana's food traditions and ingredient selection with insider knowledge.

Hands-On Ghanaian Home Cooking Lessons

In a welcoming home kitchen in Accra, you'll master traditional Ghanaian cooking methods passed down through generations of family cooks. Select and learn to prepare beloved Ghanaian dishes including jollof rice, banku, fufu, and traditional stews using authentic home cooking techniques. The cooking classes focus on genuine methods - from grinding spices by hand to achieving the perfect flavor balance that defines real Ghanaian home cooking.

Cultural Stories & Culinary Passion in Ghana

Experience the warmth of Ghanaian hospitality while learning from someone who has dedicated her life to mastering Ghana's culinary traditions. Hear stories of learning from family elders, experimenting with regional variations across Ghana, and bringing authentic flavors to restaurant kitchens in Accra.
